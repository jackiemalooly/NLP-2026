## Access to Google Colab (preferred)
Please visit [https://colab.research.google.com/](https://colab.research.google.com/) and sign in with your Gmail ID. <br/>
You should have access to a T4 GPU and a v5e-1 TPU on a free account.

## Access to SageMaker
Please visit [https://studiolab.sagemaker.aws/](https://studiolab.sagemaker.aws/) and request a free account. <br/>
After you verify the email account, you shall be added to a waitlist and will get access to Sagemaker Lab soon.
